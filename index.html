Help Move with WASD-keys. Collect all 100 points and avoid the cylinders. You can also start the game by pressing R, and toggle music/effects by pressing 1 or 2 respectively. A new username can be selected by pressing enter when in the text field. To choose your username, write it in the username field and press the 'Enter' key or click outside the field. Level selection affects the speed of the ghosts.
